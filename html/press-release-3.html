AVANCE Sports to Host Nike EYBL Scholastic in La Jolla, CA

La Jolla, CA - February 10, 2025 – AVANCE Sports is excited to announce the Nike EYBL Scholastic Event in collaboration with member, Veritas Academy (CA). Scheduled for February 22-23, 2025, at the state-of-the-art facilities of Bishop's High School in La Jolla, California, this prominent basketball showcase will comprise some of the country's top basketball players and programs. Streamed live on League Ready YouTube, Nike EYBL Scholastic promises an exciting atmosphere and an opportunity for the Southern California basketball community to connect and experience the highest caliber of scholastic competition. Recognized nationally for prioritizing education and developing elite basketball talent, the following teams will be participating:


  • #10 CIA Bella Vista (Arizona)
  • Oak Hill Academy (Virginia)
  • Veritas Academy (California)
  • # 12 Wasatch Academy (Utah)

Why It Matters
“AVANCE Sports is honored to support Nike EYBL Scholastic and bring four nationally recognized programs to San Diego,” said Gary Acosta, Founder of AVANCE Sports. “This event is a celebration of talent, hard work, and passion for the game of basketball".


EYBL Scholastic is sponsored by Nike, with Gatorade and Chipotle as additional corporate partners.
